About Jua Sounds

Jua Sounds is a North Wales reggae soundsystem and production company dedicated to bringing authentic reggae culture and positive vibrations to events of all sizes.

Established in 2024, we've been building our reputation on quality sound and a deep respect for reggae music and its roots. Our team combines technical expertise with a passion for reggae culture.

Whether you're organizing a festival, club night, private party, or corporate event, we provide the equipment, expertise, and musical selection to create an unforgettable experience.
